About gr8graphix

Design, made personal.

A studio built around life's milestones

gr8graphix is a custom graphic design business focused on one thing: turning the moments that matter most into art you can hang on your wall. Every poster we make starts as a blank canvas and is designed from scratch around the details you give us — your baby's first hours, or the years of work behind a graduate's diploma.

We believe keepsakes should feel personal, not printed from a template. That's why every order goes through a real design process — your details, your colors, your story — finished by hand and sent back to you as a proof before it's ever printed.
